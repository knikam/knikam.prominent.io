About us

Prominent Spoken English and Soft Skill Training Institute (An ISO 9001:2008 Certified Organization) was established with an underlying principle of uniform linguistic study and inculcation. We firmly believe that anybody can learn with the aid of a good trainer, and we only work with some of the best English language trainers. Join our network where more than 20,000 students have already learned to communicate in English with our online structured courses. Through the years our growth has been in many facets of Soft Skills and Spoken English Training and Development. We aim to make you succeed in every field by our outstanding proven methodologies where learning English and developing soft skills is just a piece of cake!

Our Vision

  • The vision of prominent is to enhance practice and promote the language in order to empower them to take a giant leap in their professional life.
  • To be recognized as an expert & proficient institute in training & enhancing the skill of every individual in multi-linguistic society.
  • To be a leader in providing quality training solutions to every individual.

Our Mission

  • Your excellence is our zest
  • Enriching the communication skills for personal development to stand out in competitive world.
  • To be one-stop learning partner for everyone.
