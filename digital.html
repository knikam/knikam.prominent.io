Digital Marketing

    The world is progressing digitally. A well-established digital presence is a key to success in this mobile thriving world. There is so much you can do in the virtual world to gather people’s attention. But you really must choose the best option out of the bouquet to enhance brands. The world is right on people’s fingers, but it is surely moving fast. The digital marketing certification course at Prominent is designed as per the industry standards and requirements. With wide range of subjects across different domains of technology and marketing, become a pioneer of the online media.
What you'll learn
  • Components of digital marketing, digital marketing process framework and what it takes to create a strong digital presence.
  • Creating website and webpages, buying, and hosting web domain and curating the web content
  • Managing performances, tracking conversions, and understanding the world of digital advertisement using Google Adwords. Create your own advertising campaigns, manage your ad extensions and learn how to remarket.
  • Analyzing the lead conversions and importance of web analytics for a optimized digital marketing strategy.
  • Reaching targeted and wider audience through social medias and creating an effective and prompt social media presence to interact with potential onlookers.
  • Understand different ways of creating an online content and various portals to publish and curate the content.
    Digital marketing is a vast domain with hundreds of options available to connect with your desired audience and enhance the brand visibility. The major concern is to choose the right platform and to create a seamless and authentic virtual experience for the consumers.
